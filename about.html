About Australian Fauna

Celebrating, understanding and protecting the extraordinary wildlife of Australia

Our Purpose

We exist to share the wonder of Australia’s unique animals and inspire greater care for their future.

Why this matters

Australia is home to more unique mammal, bird and reptile species than almost any other country. From the platypus and echidna — the only egg-laying mammals on Earth — to colourful parrots, deadly snakes, ancient lungfish and the world’s largest monotypic family of marsupials, our wildlife is truly one-of-a-kind.

Yet many of these species face serious threats: habitat loss, climate change, invasive species, and road mortality. Knowledge is the first step toward protection.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, engaging and accessible information about Australian native animals — so that more people fall in love with them, understand them, and want to help protect them.

  • Accurate, up-to-date species information
  • Beautiful photography and identification help
  • Conservation status and current threats
  • Stories that connect people emotionally to wildlife
  • Resources for teachers, students and wildlife enthusiasts

Who We Are

A small, independent team of naturalists, photographers, ecologists and educators who all share a deep love for Australia’s wildlife.

A volunteer-powered project

We are not a large organisation or government body. This site is created and maintained by passionate individuals who donate their time, photographs, research and writing.

We welcome contributions — high-quality wildlife photos, field observations, corrections, and guest articles — from anywhere in Australia.
